## How to use these videos

1. **First pass - John Savill AZ-204 Study Cram** (one sitting). Use the latest version on his channel.
2. **Second pass - Savill's AZ-204 Master Class** for any topic where you didn't feel confident (compute, storage, security, monitor, connect).
3. **Third pass - Azure Friday** episodes on Functions, App Service, Cosmos DB, and Service Bus for real-world demos.
4. Then take the **Practice Assessment** before scheduling the real exam.

> Tip: AZ-204 questions almost always include 2-3 *technically correct* options. Focus on the **best-fit SDK call, binding, or config** for the scenario.
